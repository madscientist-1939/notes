Introduction to Mathematics
============================

-> Math is everywhere! It is the language of the universe itself, describing how all
objects move. It governs everything from science to economics.

-> In a more physical sense, Math cannot be found anywhere, in a sense that an object
in the real world can. Math is in a more abstract 'dimension', but it still is the
language the universe uses to express things.

-> Math is inherent in reality. Objects in the night sky obey Mathematical models of
motion, wether we understand them or not. Which means, the Mathematics behind the 
universe is there, wether we know or not. This also means that Math was here way
before us(humans).

-> Mathematics: the study of questions that have definite answers.
[[ In other words: the study of questions that can be represented with one or more
actual answers, instead of being of a more 'interpreted' nature, such as History,
Sociology and so on. Math isn't subjective. ]]

-> There is a discussion that talk about if Mathematics is discovered or created. 
Some people do believe that Mathematics is purely discovered, i.e. Mathematical 
truths were always in the universe to be discovered, and we(humans) eventually 
deduced it. Other people believe that Mathematical is purely a human invention, that
have an unusually applicability in the universe. It is an interesting phylosophycal
debate.
